Nii-Kwame's Research Project

Stress Analysis via EEG and fNiRS Data Collection

The research project was for me to determine how EEG and fNIRS data collection can determine one's stress levels. For EEGs I along with my team of peers observed the five frequencies of brain waves and for fNIRS we measured two types of hemoglobin levels and through using a series of games, audio, and other trials I can determine how and what impacts a person's stress levels.
